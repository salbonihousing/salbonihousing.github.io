Founder & Creative Director

Salboni Apartment · Belgachia

Where stillness meets the rhythm
of passing trains

Salboni Apartment stands quietly beside a pond in Belgachia — a place where mornings unfold with soft reflections on water and the distant cadence of railway lines. It is a setting shaped not by noise, but by contrast: motion and pause, urban edge and intimate calm.

Life here moves with a subtle rhythm. The pond gathers light through the day, while the nearby tracks remind you of a city always in motion — close, yet never overwhelming. It is a space designed for those who value balance: connection without chaos, presence without excess.

Setting
Pondside living with open skies and natural light.
Surroundings
Belgachia’s familiar neighbourhood with easy urban access.
Atmosphere
A quiet retreat shaped by water, greenery, and gentle movement.
Living
Thoughtful spaces for everyday comfort and calm.
